Who are we?

Welcome to Apex Adventures! Our seasoned team, united by a shared love for marine life and a commitment to safety, specialises in encounters with the world's most iconic predator- the great white shark.

Founded by marine enthusiasts with backgrounds in marine biology, conservation, and adventure tourism, Apex Adventures provides a thrilling platform to explore great white sharks in their natural habitat.

Our expert guides, certified and rigorously trained in marine biology, shark behaviour, and safety, ensure unforgettable yet secure encounters. Dive with us for an unforgettable experience, creating memories that will last a lifetime.


Meet the team

Dr. Olivia Mitchell, Founder and Marine Biologist at Apex Adventures. With a Ph.D. in marine biology, Olivia's lifelong passion for the ocean led to the establishment of our company, where she combines expertise and commitment to marine conservation.

Captain James Reynolds, our Head Guide and Safety Expert, boasts over a decade of experience leading shark diving expeditions. His extensive knowledge of shark behaviour ensures both the safety and thrill of every adventure, making him an invaluable leader in our team.

Emma Carter, our Conservationist and Dive Educator, blends her love for the ocean with a commitment to conservation. With a background in environmental education, Emma leads our initiatives to inspire appreciation for marine ecosystems during our shark cage diving experiences.
